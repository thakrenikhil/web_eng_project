What is AYUSH?

AYUSH stands for Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy, India's traditional healthcare systems. These holistic practices focus on natural healing, well-being, and preventive healthcare.

✔️ Ayurveda: Herbal medicine & detoxification. ✔️ Yoga: Physical & mental wellness through asanas & meditation. ✔️ Unani: Ancient Greco-Arabic healing techniques. ✔️ Siddha: Tamil-originated medicine using herbs & minerals. ✔️ Homeopathy: Natural remedies that boost the body's healing power.
